Patient Information

Everything you need to know before, during, and after your appointment with us.

What to Expect

1

Before Your Appointment

Upon confirmation of your appointment, you will receive a detailed email with all the information you need ahead of your visit.

To support a seamless and highly focused consultation, we will ask you to provide a referral letter or a brief summary of your medical history and current symptoms in advance.

It would also be helpful if you could provide a list of your current medications, along with any relevant test results or imaging, prior to your appointment.

2

During Your Consultation

Your initial consultation typically lasts approximately 30 minutes. During this time, your consultant will take a comprehensive medical history, discuss your symptoms in detail, and, where clinically appropriate, carry out a physical examination.

Diagnostic tests or imaging may be arranged following your consultation, either on the same day where feasible or at a time that is convenient for you.

Your consultant will clearly explain any findings and guide you through the most appropriate treatment options.

3

After Your Appointment

Following your consultation, you will receive a detailed letter summarising the findings and recommended treatment plan. With your consent, this will also be shared with your GP and any other relevant healthcare professionals.

Our team will arrange any follow-up appointments, tests, or procedures as discussed with your consultant.

If you are self-funding, our team will provide a written quotation for any recommended tests or procedures, ensuring you are fully informed before you choose to proceed.

Insurance & Payment

Private Medical Insurance

We are recognised by all major private medical insurers.

Before your appointment, please contact your insurer to obtain authorisation. Once confirmed, email your policy number and authorisation code to your consultant's secretary or accounts@londonurologyspecialists.co.uk.

Please also bring these details with you on the day of your appointment.

Self-Pay Patients

If you are paying for your own treatment, we offer clear and transparent pricing.

New patient consultations £340
Follow-up appointments £230

Should any diagnostic tests or procedures be required, we or the hospital will confirm the associated costs with you before proceeding.

We accept payment by credit/debit card or bank transfer.

Frequently Asked Questions

A GP referral is not required for private consultations, although we recommend having one as it provides valuable background on your medical history.

If you are using private medical insurance, your insurer may require a referral letter for authorisation.

We aim to offer appointments within 24–48 hours of your enquiry. Where clinically appropriate, same-day appointments may also be available.

Please contact our team to check current availability.

Please bring:

  • A list of your current medications
  • Any relevant test results or imaging (e.g. scans, X-rays, blood tests)
  • Your referral letter, if you have one
  • Your insurance details (if applicable)

We recommend arriving 15 minutes early to allow time for registration.

Where appropriate, simple tests such as blood or urine tests may be arranged on the same day as your consultation.

Imaging and procedures are typically scheduled for a separate appointment, allowing for appropriate planning and giving you the flexibility to choose a convenient date and time.

  • New patient consultations will not exceed £340
  • Follow-up appointments will not exceed £230

If you are using private medical insurance, please check with your insurer to confirm your cover and any authorisation requirements.

While we don't have dedicated parking, there are several NCP car parks within walking distance, including Cavendish Square and Marylebone Road. The clinic is also easily accessible by public transport, with Regent's Park and Oxford Circus tube stations nearby.
