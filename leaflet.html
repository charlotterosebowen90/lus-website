Clinical expertise
Services
Prostate Conditions
  • Comprehensive prostate cancer evaluation and treatment options
  • Assessment and monitoring of elevated PSA levels
  • Management of benign prostatic hyperplasia (BPH)
  • Diagnosis and treatment of prostatitis
  • Care for post-treatment prostate complications
Kidney & Bladder
  • Assessment and treatment of kidney and bladder cancers
  • Investigation of visible or microscopic haematuria
  • Evaluation and management of overactive bladder
  • Management of urinary incontinence
  • Diagnosis and ongoing care for recurrent urinary tract infections
Stone Management & Treatment
  • Assessment and treatment of renal, ureteric, and bladder calculi
  • Investigation and management of acute renal colic
  • Endoscopic stone interventions: ureteroscopy, flexible ureterorenoscopy, and PCNL with special interest in local anaesthetic procedures for high risk anaesthetic patients.
  • Shockwave lithotripsy (SWL) for upper tract stones
  • Metabolic stone evaluation and targeted medical therapy
  • Management of recurrent and complex calculi, including staghorn stones
  • Ongoing surveillance to reduce recurrence risk
Men’s Health
  • Vasectomy and vasectomy-related care
  • Assessment and management of testicular conditions
  • Diagnosis and treatment of varicocele and hydrocele
  • Adult circumcision procedures
  • Evaluation and management of erectile dysfunction
Women’s Urology
  • Stress and urge urinary incontinence
  • Pelvic organ prolapse
  • Bladder pain syndrome
  • Surgical options for urinary continence
  • Female urological reconstructive procedures
Advanced Surgery
  • Robotic-assisted urological procedures
  • Minimally invasive approaches
  • Urological reconstructive surgery
  • Urinary diversion for benign and malignant conditions
  • Complex urological cancer surgery
Diagnostic Services
  • Multiparametric prostate MRI
  • MRI-guided targeted prostate biopsy
  • PSMA PET imaging for prostate cancer assessment
  • CT and ultrasound for urological evaluation
  • Comprehensive urodynamic studies
