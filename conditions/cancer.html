Cancer Clinic

Comprehensive Cancer Care

Our Cancer Clinic provides expert diagnosis, treatment, and ongoing care for patients with urological cancers. We understand that a cancer diagnosis can be overwhelming, and we are committed to providing clear guidance and support throughout.

Our consultants are leaders in their field, offering access to advanced diagnostics and a full range of treatment options, including minimally invasive and robotic surgery.

We work closely with oncologists, radiologists, histopathologists, nuclear medicine consultants, and specialist nurses to ensure coordinated, multidisciplinary care at every stage of your treatment.

What We Treat

Conditions We Treat

Prostate Cancer

The most common cancer in men. We offer comprehensive care, from initial assessment and diagnosis through to treatment, including active surveillance programmes, focal therapies, and surgery.

  • ›PSA testing and monitoring
  • ›3T multiparametric MRI imaging
  • ›MRI-guided prostate biopsy
  • ›Active surveillance programmes
  • ›Focal therapies (HIFU, cryotherapy, NanoKnife)
  • ›Robotic prostatectomy
Find out more

Bladder Cancer

Expert care across all stages of bladder cancer, from early superficial tumours to advanced disease requiring complex management, including reconstructive surgery.

  • ›Flexible cystoscopy
  • ›TURBT (transurethral resection of bladder tumour)
  • ›Intravesical BCG immunotherapy
  • ›Radical cystectomy
Find out more

Kidney Cancer

Comprehensive care for kidney tumours, including active surveillance for small tumours and kidney-sparing surgery where appropriate to preserve renal function.

  • ›Active surveillance for small tumours
  • ›Partial nephrectomy (kidney-sparing surgery)
  • ›Robotic surgery
Find out more

Testicular Cancer

Rapid assessment and treatment for testicular cancer, the most common cancer in young men, with excellent outcomes when diagnosed early.

  • ›Urgent ultrasound assessment
  • ›Tumour marker blood tests
  • ›Orchidectomy (testicle removal surgery)
Find out more
Our Approach

Why Choose Our Cancer Clinic?

MDT-Led Care

Every case reviewed by a multidisciplinary team of specialists including oncologists, radiologists, and pathologists.

Advanced Diagnostics

3T MRI, targeted biopsy, PSMA PET-CT, and specialist imaging for precise diagnosis and staging.

Minimally Invasive

Robotic surgery, focal therapy, and organ-preserving approaches to reduce recovery time.

Consultant-Led

Senior specialist oversight from diagnosis through to follow-up, ensuring continuity of care at every stage.
