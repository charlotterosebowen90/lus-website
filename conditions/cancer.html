Cancer Clinic

Expert diagnosis and treatment of urological cancers, delivered with a compassionate, evidence-based approach.

Comprehensive Cancer Care

Our Cancer Clinic provides expert diagnosis, treatment, and ongoing care for patients with urological cancers. We understand that a cancer diagnosis can be overwhelming, and we are committed to providing clear guidance and support throughout.

Our consultants are leaders in their field, offering access to advanced diagnostics and a full range of treatment options, including minimally invasive and robotic surgery.

We work closely with oncologists, radiologists, histopathologists, nuclear medicine consultants, and specialist nurses to ensure coordinated, multidisciplinary care at every stage of your treatment.

Conditions We Treat

Prostate Cancer

The most common cancer in men. We offer comprehensive care, from initial assessment and diagnosis through to treatment, including active surveillance programmes, focal therapies, and surgery. Where appropriate, we arrange onward referral for radiotherapy.

  • PSA testing and monitoring
  • 3T multiparametric MRI imaging
  • MRI-guided prostate biopsy
  • Active surveillance programmes
  • Focal therapies, including High-Intensity Focused Ultrasound (HIFU), focal cryotherapy, and Irreversible Electroporation (NanoKnife)
  • Robotic prostatectomy
Find out more

Bladder Cancer

We provide expert care across all stages of bladder cancer, from early superficial tumours to more advanced disease requiring complex management, including reconstructive surgery where appropriate.

  • Flexible cystoscopy
  • Transurethral resection of bladder tumour (TURBT)
  • Intravesical BCG immunotherapy
  • Radical cystectomy (bladder removal surgery)
Find out more

Kidney Cancer

We provide comprehensive care for kidney tumours, including active surveillance for small tumours and a focus on kidney-sparing surgery where appropriate to preserve renal function.

  • Active surveillance for small tumours
  • Partial nephrectomy (kidney-sparing surgery)
  • Robotic surgery
Find out more

Testicular Cancer

We provide rapid assessment and treatment for testicular cancer, the most common cancer in young men, with excellent outcomes when diagnosed early.

  • Urgent ultrasound assessment
  • Tumour marker blood tests
  • Orchidectomy (testicle removal surgery)
