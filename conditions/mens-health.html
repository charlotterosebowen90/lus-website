Men's Health

Specialized care for men's urological conditions, delivered with discretion and expertise.

Dedicated Men's Health Clinic

Our Men's Health Clinic provides comprehensive care for the full range of urological conditions affecting men. We understand that discussing these issues can feel uncomfortable, which is why we prioritize discretion and a supportive environment.

From common conditions like enlarged prostate to more complex issues affecting sexual health and fertility, our specialists have the expertise to diagnose and treat effectively.

We take time to understand your concerns, explain your options clearly, and work with you to develop a treatment plan that fits your lifestyle and goals.

Conditions We Treat

Erectile Dysfunction

A common condition affecting men of all ages. We offer comprehensive assessment to identify underlying causes and provide effective treatments.

  • Thorough medical assessment
  • Medication therapy
  • Injection therapy
  • Surgical options when appropriate

Enlarged Prostate (BPH)

Benign prostatic hyperplasia affects most men as they age, causing urinary symptoms. We offer the latest treatments to restore quality of life.

  • Urodynamic testing
  • Medication management
  • Minimally invasive procedures (UroLift, Rezum)
  • TURP and laser surgery

Male Fertility

Comprehensive fertility assessment and treatment for couples struggling to conceive, addressing male factor infertility.

  • Semen analysis
  • Hormone testing
  • Varicocele repair
  • Vasectomy reversal

Other Conditions

We also treat a wide range of other men's health conditions including:

  • Peyronie's disease
  • Low testosterone
  • Chronic prostatitis
  • Vasectomy
