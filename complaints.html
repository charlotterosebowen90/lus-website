Patient Feedback

Feedback & Complaints

We are committed to providing the highest standard of care. If your experience has not met your expectations, we want to hear from you.

Our Promise

Our Commitment

We take all feedback seriously and view complaints as an opportunity to learn and improve. We are committed to handling every concern fairly, thoroughly, and in a timely manner. Whether your feedback is positive or highlights an area for improvement, we welcome it.

General Feedback

How to Provide Feedback

We welcome all feedback about your experience with us. If you would like to share positive feedback or a suggestion for improvement, you can do so by emailing info@londonurologyspecialists.co.uk or speaking to any member of our team.

Positive feedback is shared with the relevant team members and helps us understand what we are doing well.

What outcome you are seeking

Our Process

What Happens Next

Our complaints process follows these steps:

1. Acknowledgement

We will acknowledge receipt of your complaint in writing within 48 business hours.

2. Investigation

Your complaint will be investigated thoroughly and fairly. We may need to review clinical records and speak with the staff involved.

3. Response

We aim to provide a full written response within 20 business days. If the investigation requires more time, we will write to you explaining the reason for the delay and provide a revised timeline.

4. Resolution

Our response will address each point raised in your complaint and outline any actions we intend to take.

Escalation

If You Are Not Satisfied

If you are not satisfied with our response, you may:

Request a review of the complaint by a senior member of the team

Seek independent advice or raise your concern with the relevant professional regulatory body (such as the General Medical Council) if your complaint relates to the conduct of an individual clinician

Your Privacy

Confidentiality

All complaints are handled in strict confidence. Making a complaint will not affect your ongoing or future care with us in any way.

Information relating to your complaint will be stored securely and in accordance with our Privacy Policy.
