About Mr Nobrega

Mr Richard Nobrega is a Consultant Urological Surgeon at University College London Hospitals (UCLH) and the Clinical Lead for Neuro-Urology at the Royal National Orthopaedic Hospital (RNOH), which hosts one of the largest specialist spinal injury units in the United Kingdom.

He holds the position of Co-Chair for Female and Reconstructive Urology services across North Central London, bringing together multidisciplinary expertise to improve patient outcomes in this complex field. He was appointed as a substantive consultant in 2022.

Mr Nobrega is dedicated to the holistic management of patients with neurological conditions affecting the urinary tract, and he has particular expertise in helping individuals who experience urinary incontinence following cancer treatment, including surgery and radiotherapy. His approach combines clinical excellence with a deep commitment to patient-centred care.
