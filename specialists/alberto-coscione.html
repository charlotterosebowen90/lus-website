About

Expert Stone Surgeon & Endourologist

Mr Alberto Coscione is a Chelsea-based Consultant Urological Surgeon with specialist expertise in endourology, stone surgery, and core urological conditions such as benign prostatic hyperplasia (BPH). A graduate of Cambridge University Medical School, he brings together academic distinction and extensive clinical training gained across some of London's and the East of England's most respected NHS institutions.

His surgical training spanned Chelsea and Westminster Hospital, Addenbrooke's Hospital Cambridge, Charing Cross Hospital, and St George's Hospital, providing him with broad exposure to complex urological cases and advanced surgical techniques. He achieved his FRCS in Urology in 2022 and holds recognition as an Associate Fellow of the Higher Education Academy, reflecting his commitment to both clinical excellence and the education of future surgeons.

Mr Coscione is a pioneer of local anaesthetic stone surgery, having developed dedicated services in both Cambridge and London. Fluent in English, Italian, and Greek, and with a working knowledge of French, he is exceptionally well placed to deliver outstanding, patient-centred care in a multicultural setting.
