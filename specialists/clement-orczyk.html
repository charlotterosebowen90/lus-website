About

A Career Spanning Three Countries

Professor Clément Orczyk is a Clinical Academic and Consultant Urologist at University College London Hospitals (UCLH), where he also holds the position of Associate Professor of Urology at University College London (UCL). He has served as a consultant since 2017 and currently leads the Prostate Cancer Unit for diagnostics, active surveillance, and focal therapy at UCLH.

His career has taken him across France, the United States, and the United Kingdom. He completed a prestigious fellowship at New York University (NYU) in 2011 and subsequently undertook a PhD focusing on prostate cancer imaging and focal therapy at a leading French research institution, conducted in collaboration with NYU.

This international perspective, combined with decades of clinical and academic experience, uniquely positions Professor Orczyk at the forefront of prostate cancer care and innovation.

Focal Therapy Expertise

A Global Authority in Focal Therapy

Professor Orczyk is recognised internationally as one of a select group of clinicians proficient in multiple forms of focal ablation for prostate cancer. His procedural experience is extensive, having performed over 700 focal therapy procedures to date.

HIFU

High-Intensity Focused Ultrasound for targeted tumour ablation

Cryotherapy

Controlled freezing to destroy cancerous tissue with precision

Nanoknife (IRE)

Irreversible electroporation for tumours near critical structures

700+ Focal Therapy Procedures

Professor Orczyk's substantial procedural volume reflects both his expertise and the trust placed in him by patients and referring clinicians across the UK and internationally. His proficiency across multiple ablation modalities is rare globally, offering patients a tailored approach matched to their individual tumour characteristics.

Research & Publications

Advancing the Science of Prostate Cancer

Professor Orczyk is a prolific researcher whose work sits at the intersection of imaging science, molecular biology, and artificial intelligence. He authored the first publication to establish three-dimensional treatment margins for focal therapy — a foundational contribution that has shaped clinical practice worldwide.

His translational research programme bridges the gap between laboratory discovery and bedside application, with a particular focus on how advanced imaging and AI can improve diagnostic accuracy and treatment planning in prostate cancer. He publishes regularly in high-impact, peer-reviewed journals and is a sought-after speaker at international urological conferences.

Pioneering Publications

First to define 3D treatment margins for focal therapy, informing global clinical standards.

AI & Imaging Innovation

Translational research combining imaging, molecular biology, and artificial intelligence.

Recognition

Honours & Distinctions

In recognition of his contributions to the field and his role in fostering collaboration between France and the United Kingdom, Professor Orczyk was appointed to the Young Leader Programme of the Franco-British Council — a distinction reserved for professionals demonstrating exceptional promise and impact in their respective disciplines.

Franco-British Council Young Leader

Selected for the Young Leader Programme — recognising outstanding international professional achievement and cross-border collaboration.
